Why ArcConnect

Built for real mentorship, not just networking

🎯

Structured opportunities

Every mentorship has a defined scope, clear criteria, and a stated goal — so both mentor and student know exactly what to expect before they begin.

⚡

Fast, transparent matching

Apply in seconds, not hours. Mentors review and respond quickly, and students are never left guessing — every status update is visible in real time.

🔒

Role-based access control

Mentors manage only the opportunities they create. Students apply and track their own progress. Every action is permission-gated, so nothing falls into the wrong hands.

📋

Full lifecycle management

From posting an opportunity to accepting applicants to completing the mentorship — every stage is tracked and fully managed inside the platform, with nothing falling through the cracks.

🌐

Multi-domain coverage

Placements, higher studies, internships, entrepreneurship and more — alumni from every career path are here, ready to guide students toward whichever direction they choose to pursue.

📊

Real-time dashboards

Mentors and students each get a tailored dashboard built for their role. No clutter, no irrelevant data — only the actions and information that actually matter to you.

The process

From opportunity to outcome — three clear steps

01
Alumni

Post a mentorship opportunity

Define the domain, participation criteria, number of available slots, and what students can genuinely expect to gain from working with you.

02
Student

Discover, review, and apply

Browse open opportunities by domain or keyword, read the full details, check whether you meet the criteria, and apply with a single click.

03
Both

Connect and grow together

Mentors accept the right applicants, students get notified instantly, and the mentorship begins — with progress fully tracked and visible on both sides.
